PANCHAGAVYA

Panchgavya refers to the milk, urine, dung, ghee, and curd; derived from Indian Desi cow (A 2 Indian Breed). In Ayurveda, nutritional and medicinal properties of individual component of Panchgavya has been described in detail. Several research studies have reported and validated the health benefits of Panchgavya.

Go-dugdha (milk of Indian Desi cow): It has the qualities that alleviates the diseases caused by vitiation of Vata and Pitta in the body. It is good for heart. It improves immunity and longivity. It is indicated in the prevention and treatment of various physical and mental disorders.

Go-mutra (urine of Indian Desi cow): It is helpful in the treatment of diseases like obesity, heart diseases, kidney diseases, anaemia, jaundice, Diabetes mellitus, gastrointestinal diseases, respiratory tract diseases, skin diseases and wounds etc.

Gobar (dung of Indian Desi cow): It is the seat of Goddess Lakshmi. It has the properties of purifying the environment and is used for sacred purposes. Dried cowdung is used as the source of energy for domestic use and to prepare various Ayurvedic medicines. A good number of cowdung based health care products are available like tooth care products, skin care products and eye care products etc. These products have a high demand as they are natural and free from harmful chemicals.

Go-ghrita (Ghee of Indian Desi cow): It is good for eyesight and has detoxifying properties. It is good for diseases caused by vitiation of Vata and Pitta in the body. It improves memory and strength. It is used in a wide variety of Ayurvedic medicines and tonics like Chavanprasha, Brahmi Ghrita, Shatdhauta Ghrita, Triphala Ghrita etc. It contains essential fatty acids and fat soluble vitamins like A,D,E,K.

Go-dadhi (Curd of Indian Desi cow): It is good for anorexia and indigestion. It is useful in diseases arising due to vitiation of Vata Dosha. It improves digestive fire and physical strength.

Cowpathy: It is Panchgavya based treatment of multiple diseases. It is highly advocated by Ayurveda owing to its properties that are natural and free from adverse side effects.
