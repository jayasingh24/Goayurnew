AYURVEDA

Ayurveda is an ancient health science and traditional system of medicine serving humanity since antiquity. It deals with prevention and treatment of diseases. For the ailments that are difficult to cure or are incurable, preventive measures are very important.

Ayurveda tells about how to protect health by following healthy life style, dietary management, practice of Yoga and Pranayama and regular detoxification of body by procedures of Panchakarma. For treatment of diseases, the concept of Shudhha Chikitsa is followed in Ayurveda.

Shudhha Chikitsa is the treatment that tends to cure the disease for which it is used, at the same time does not produce adverse effects or any other disease. Now a days, it is seen that the medicines that are prescribed for treating a disease have so many adverse effects that they produce other diseases in an attempt to bring some relief in the previous one. Especially for the diseases that require a treatment of long duration, Shudhha Chikitsa of Ayurveda is an ideal and rational treatment.
